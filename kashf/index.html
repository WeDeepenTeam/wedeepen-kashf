Sarah Solstice

Lead Performer & Ceremonial Guide

Sarah entered human living through Mennonite and Apostolic influence in the midwest. No outside media. Expression about religion in the form of music, art, and comedy was allowed. Amplified praise music and Gregorian hymns blasted across the property. She began hearing and writing songs about love & heaven.

Around age 8, Sarah and her pet squirrel discovered MTV. The question arose: “Where are these dance parties taking place?” She made it her main goal to find these gatherings, with plans to sing and dance on the perpetual. “Just like in real life,” she’d say.

By 18 she was singing at Universal Studios and flying to Maui to perform on island stages. At 20 she became the opening act for Britney Spears’ Dream Within a Dream tour—the crowd grew to over 30,000 daily. She toured with athletes on the Children’s Go For It Roadshow, filmed television on USA & ABC Family, walked the red carpet at the Grammy Awards as a Columbia Recording artist, and opened for Jessica Simpson, OutKast, and others.

But no one was talking about real life—Hmm…

Ultimately unfulfilled & feeling empty from the messages inside not being shared with these mega crowds, she exited the scene through a string of miracles that her mother still talks about. She moved to Hawaii to immerse herself in yoga & the study of world religions. Heaven was the syllabus. Truths began to reveal themselves in song & nursery rhyme format. When full music compositions began to play in her dreams, she decided to get serious about creating what she was hearing.

KASHF is that dream made real—a world where the party and the prayer are the same thing, where the body speaks its first language, and where 30,000 strangers become 20 people who actually see each other.

Practical Details

What should I expect?
An overnight experience from 7pm through the morning. The evening program closes around 2am, with an optional overnight stay and morning closing circle from 9–11am. Expect to be moved, held, and seen in ways that shift your understanding of the feminine.
What about the sacrament?
Sacrament is available for those who are interested. Please complete the intake form following registration. This is required and important to do.
Where is the event held?
The event is held at a private venue in the Austin, TX 78738 area. Full address and arrival details are shared with registered guests.
What is the refund policy?
Due to the intimate nature and limited capacity, all sales are final. Tickets may be transferred to another guest with advance notice.
What should I wear?
Dress in what makes you feel sovereign. White or earth tones are welcomed but not required. Comfort is essential for an overnight experience.
Who is this for?
KASHF welcomes women, men, couples, and friends—anyone 18+ who honors the feminine and feels called to this experience. All are held here.
